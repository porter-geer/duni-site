export const operations = {
  headline: "How DUNI operates",
  body: `DUNI is administered by two roles defined in its Association Agreement:

**Administrator (Cowrie):** Responsible for tax compliance, financial statements, banking, and maintaining DUNI's legal existence. Based in Cheyenne, Wyoming, Cowrie provides legal, financial, and administrative support to decentralized protocols. The Administrator has no discretion over governance decisions — all management decisions remain with DUNI members through onchain governance.

**Ministerial Agent (Uniswap Foundation):** A limited, well-defined role to facilitate administrative tasks on behalf of DUNI without introducing centralized control. The Ministerial Agent executes documents, coordinates with service providers, and transmits governance-approved funding — all within the explicit bounds of governance-approved instructions.`,
